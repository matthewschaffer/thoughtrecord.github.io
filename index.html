Thought Record

Interactive worksheet for examining and challenging thoughts

Remember: Thinking something doesn't make it true!

Instructions: If you notice your mood getting worse, or find yourself doing unhelpful behaviors (including procrastination!), ask yourself: "What's going through my mind right now?" Then, complete this thought record to examine and challenge your thoughts. Spend just 5-10 minutes on this exercise. Remember: If your mood improves by 10% or more, it was worth doing this worksheet.
NOTE: What you write here stays here. It is private. There is no save function.

Adaptive Response

Use the questions below to compose a response to the automatic thought(s)

Questions to consider:
1. What is the evidence that the automatic thought is true? Not true?
2. Is there an alternative explanation for the thought?
3. If the worst happened, how could you cope? What's the best that could happen? What's the most realistic outcome?
4. What's the effect of believing the automatic thought? What could be the effect of changing your thinking?
5. If [person's name] was in this situation and had this thought, what would you tell them?
