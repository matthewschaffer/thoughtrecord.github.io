Thought Record

Interactive worksheet for examining and challenging thoughts

Just because you think something, doesn't necessarily mean it's true.

Instructions: When you notice your mood getting worse or find yourself engaging in unhelpful behavior, ask yourself "What's going through my mind right now?" Complete this thought record to examine and challenge your thoughts. Spend just 5-10 minutes on this exercise. Remember: It was worth doing this worksheet if your mood improves by 10% or more.

Questions to Help Compose an Alternative Response

(1) What is the evidence that the automatic thought is true? Not true?
(2) Is there an alternative explanation?
(3) If the worst happened, how could I cope? What's the best that could happen? What's the most realistic outcome?
(4) What's the effect of my believing the automatic thought? What could be the effect of my changing my thinking?
(5) If [person's name] was in this situation and had this thought, what would I tell them?
(6) What would be good to do?
